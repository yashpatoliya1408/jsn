Swaminaryan bhagwan charitray


This is the third part of the animation film series on Bhagwan Swaminarayan. We follow His studies in Ayodhya as the child Ghanshyam, son of Dharmadev and Bhaktimata. His knowledge and understanding of the scriptures astounds even the scholars of Kashi. His divine playfulness makes the villagefolk realise the importance of an honest life and the fruitlessness of greed. His miracles as a child – whether appearing in many mandirs at same time or having the devtas’ physicians come to his aid – reveal his divinity to friends and family. Everyone in Ayodhya is touched and affected by His aura. But once His duty to his parents is done, he sets his sights westward, where his devotees await. He leaves home to adopt the world as his family, setting out on his objective of redeeming all whom He meets.
